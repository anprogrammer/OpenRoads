F.A.Q.

Q: What is the Jump-o Master?

A: The Jump-o Master is a simple AI, part of your ship, which assists you on your mission. If it detects that you are going to fall into nothingness, it can instantly alter your speed so that you don't undershoot or overshoot your landing.

It has limitations in how much it can alter your speed, and also won't save you from crashing into a block or tunnel.

Q: Why won't my Xbox 360 controller work?

A: As a security feature, some browsers won't allow us to use your Xbox 360 controller until you press the "A" button to enable it.

Q: I found a bug. How do I report it?

A: Feel free to log an issue on the OpenRoads GitHub. I will do my best to address it.

Keep in mind that OpenRoads is open source, so if you are an included developer you can always fix the bug and submit a pull request.

Q: I'd like to make a donation.

A: You caught me, this is neither a question nor has anyone ever asked it.

Consider donating to the DosBox project instead. It's a great project run by great people (pretty much for free), and was absolutely integral to the creation of OpenRoads.
